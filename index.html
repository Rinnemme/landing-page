I think anime is great and I don't care what anyone says. This little page is showing off a few that I quite like, all of which are on Crunchyroll.
Here are some anime I love
Baccano spans multiple stories and timelines, dealing with mortality and the human condition, containing a great deal of both comedy and drama.
Fire force takes place in a world afflicted by a fiery affliction, centerin around a ragtag team attempting to get to the bottom of who is involved in creatinga and maintaining this apocalytpic status quo.
Samurai champloo centers around two samurai of opposite personalities (one princpled and stoic, the other bombastic and uninhibited) helping a young woman find someone she seeks.
Somali and the Forest Spirit is a beautiful story about a golem helping a young girl, who he found lost in his forest, find her parents, learning in the process a bit about what it is to be human.
